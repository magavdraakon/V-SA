  • On the 19th of March there was a VÕSA Project seminar held in TUT IT College for foreign students

    Mar 19, 2018

    On the 19th of March there was a seminar held in TUT IT College regarding the VÕSA project. Significant aspects about writing and defending a diploma thesis were covered.   • 19. veebruaril toimus TTÜ IT Kolledžis VÕSA projekti raames seminar tudengitele

    Feb 21, 2018

  • Tehnikaülikooli virtuaalseid õpiruume saavad kasutada ka teiste kõrg- ja kutsekoolide õppejõud
